About Us

Welcome to Homemade Homestead! We are a veteran-owned farm stand offering produce, eggs, jam, baked goods, and soaps. We love to share our passion of farming with the community and we donate a portion of our products and produce to the local food pantry.

What We Offer

  • Fresh produce (seasonal)
  • Farm-fresh eggs
  • Homemade jams
  • Baked goods (rotating selection)
  • Handcrafted soaps
